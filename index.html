About Us

Fostering The Future Of AI Talent
At The University of Waterloo


WAT.ai is a student-run Artificial Intelligence (AI) Organization at the University of Waterloo and a member of the Sedra Student Design Centre (SSDC). Our goal is to establish an environment to grow the future of AI talent at Waterloo. We provide opportunities for undergraduate and graduate students to engage in impactful projects through collaboration with companies and internal research.
